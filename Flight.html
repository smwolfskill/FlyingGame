Controls

W, ↑ : Pitch up

S, ↓ : Pitch down

A, ← : Turn left

D, → : Turn right

Q : Roll left

E : Roll right

1, + : Accelerate

3, - : Decelerate

Space: Pause

Enter: Unpause

Instructions

The goal is to fly around and collect as many spheres falling from the sky as possible. There are several types with different properties:

Silver: +1 Score. The most common sphere.

Gold: +3 Score. More rare than silver.

Pink: Lose all score; avoid these!

Green: Reverse gravity for 5 seconds, and score double points for 15 seconds! Rare.
